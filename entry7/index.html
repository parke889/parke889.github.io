Sky, My Old Friend

special note

The more we grow up, the less frequently we look up at the sky than we used to. Sometimes not even once a day. Maybe it's because we think there are already too many things to care about or handle in real life to daydream about something looking at the sky.

I remember that I always liked looking up at the sky all the time when I was little. Looking at the sky used to be another way to play because it felt like I was immersed in a whole new world when I used to imagine interesting objects, animals, or scenes looking at different shapes of clouds.

For this entry, I focused on bringing an interesting habit back from my childhood. Please have fun looking around these records of what I found from the sky this week, and sometimes look up at the sky and feel free to imagine whatever comes to your mind.
