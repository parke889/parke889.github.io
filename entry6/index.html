Same Book Different Feeling
Look what I found at a bookstore last weekend. The Rainbow Fish by Marcus Pfister, The Giviing Tree,... They were the same books as I used to enjoy, but I got totally different feeling when I, as a grown up, read them. Here are some quotes from those familiar books.
"The rainbow fish shared his scales left and right.
And the more he gave away, the more delighted he became."
The Rainbow Fish
""I am to big to climb and play." said the boy.
"I want to buy things and have fun. I want some money. Can you give me some money?""
The Giving Tree
"You have brains in your head.
You have feet in your shoes.
You can steer yourself any direction you choose."
Oh, the Places You'll Go!
